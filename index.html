Bringing Back Bedtime Stories

Over one third of adults get 6 or less hours of sleep due to stress, discomfort and restlessness. We're changing that. Bedtime stories aren't just for kids - a 2014 Oxford study found out that an adult that was read a story fell asleep 40% faster than one that wasn't. So after months of research and testing we've created over a thousand audio stories to listen to on our app free of charge designed to get you snoozing.
